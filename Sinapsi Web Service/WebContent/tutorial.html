Getting Started

Basic usage


After you downloaded Sinapsi client, follow these steps that will introduce you to the Sinapsi world

Sign In in the Sinapsi platform


If you are not already a Sinapsi user, you need to create an account. You can create the account from the client app or clicking here

Create your first macro!


Press the + button, and you will see the Macro Editor. In the Macro Editor select the Trigger that you want execute the macro, after that select the action/s that you want to be performed. Remember that actions can be perfomed also in the other devices connected to your Sinapsi account, so for each action select the device that will execute the selected action.

Macros


This is the macro manager, here you can edit, create and delete your macros and your Macros are syncronized over your devices.


Macros Syncronization

Sinapsi have a very powerful and stable macro syncronization system. The macro syncronization gives you the ability to have all your macros in all your devices. For example if you delete a macro from your smartphone and you login from your tablet after the changes, you will see that the macro deleted from your smartphone has been deleted also from the tablet.

Edit Macros


To Edit/delete macro, swipe to left over the macro and you will see the quick-menu

Enable/Disable Macros


If you want to disable a macro, all you need to do is to tap over the macro icon, easy easy.

Good work!


Now you know how to create new macros and how to edit and delete them. Sinapsi is very wide and powerful so make a good usage of it. If you have problems with Sinapsi clients please contact us.
